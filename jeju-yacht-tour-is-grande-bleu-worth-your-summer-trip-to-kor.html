Coastal Gems & Picture-Perfect Moments

Our 60-minute journey from Daepo Port in Seogwipo, Jeju, takes you past some of Jeju’s most striking coastal formations. These are views you simply can’t get from land, offering a unique perspective on the island's volcanic beauty.

You’ll get up close to iconic landmarks such as Elephant Rock (Kkokkiribawi), a natural cliff carved by the elements, and the dramatic vertical walls of Jingotnae Sea Canyon. Perhaps the most awe-inspiring are the Wolpyeong Basalt Columns (Wolpyeong Jusangjeolli), hexagonal volcanic sea cliffs reminiscent of a UNESCO Geopark site. We also pass the mysterious Wolpyeong Sea Cave and the broad, photogenic Pangpaengideok rock platform.

Keep an eye out for a truly special treat: if you're lucky, you might even spot wild bottlenose dolphins in their natural habitat, a genuinely magical moment. For the shutterbugs, the catamaran’s wide, stable deck provides ample opportunity for Instagrammable photos, particularly during the 360-degree panoramic golden hour views on our sunset cruises. Our crew are adept at helping you capture that perfect shot against Jeju's stunning backdrop.
